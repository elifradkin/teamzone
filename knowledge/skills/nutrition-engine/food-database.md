substitution — keeps it out of every prompt. All items here are kosher and
fish-free and eggplant-free by construction. Values are approximate per common
serving; use for scaling, not lab-grade accuracy.

## Fish replacements (NEVER suggest fish — use these instead)
Any plan option that originally used salmon/tuna is replaced with:
- Grilled/baked chicken or turkey breast (matching the protein grams), or
- Tofu (vegetarian/vegan users), or
- Eggs/shakshuka (lighter meals), or
- Cottage/Pro yogurt (cold/quick meals).
Pick the swap that matches the meal's role and the day's macro targets.

## Approved vegetables (3-color rule)
cucumber, tomato, gamba (bell) pepper, radish, spinach, carrot, celery, broccoli,
green beans, cauliflower, brussels sprouts, lettuce, leafy greens.
**Never:** eggplant (house rule).

## Kosher quick-reference (full rules in `kosher-rules`)
- No pork, no shellfish, no fish (house rule), no mixing meat + dairy in one meal.
- Meat meals: chicken, turkey, beef. Dairy meals: cottage, yogurt, cheeses, milk.
- Pareve (neutral): eggs, tofu, legumes, grains, vegetables, nuts, oils — combine
  with either meat or dairy.

## Practical prep tips (from the plan)
- Buy 1 kg chicken breast, bake, split into 4–5 portions in separate containers.
- Measure other items with a spoon/tablespoon — the natural way food goes on a plate.
- No calorie counting for the user: portions are by composition, the app does the math.
