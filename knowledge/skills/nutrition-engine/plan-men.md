# Team Zone Nutrition Plan — Men / תוכנית גברים

> Source: Team Zone "באנו לעשות סדר" men's guide. Digitized and normalized.
> **Fish options removed** per house rule (no fish); compliant swaps provided.
> **No eggplant** anywhere.
> Quantities are the plan's *reference* amounts. The assistant must SCALE them to
> each user via `calorie-macros.md` and the user's `targets.md`.

## Macro philosophy
- **Protein ≈ 2.5 g × bodyweight (kg)/day** — the anchor of the whole plan.
- Split emphasis: **~60% protein / ~20% carbohydrate / ~20% fat** (plate emphasis, not strict calorie accounting — total calories come from `calorie-macros.md`).
- **Plate order: protein first → vegetables/salad → carbohydrate last.**
- Preferred option in each category is **Option 1**, except on training days where the training-day option is preferred.

## Core rules (apply to every suggestion)
1. **Around training:** ~0.5–2 h before training, a balanced meal with protein + fats + complex carbs. Right before / before a morning workout (≤30 min), a fast carb as fruit (date or banana). After training, eating is optional unless feeling weak/dizzy/tired → then a full balanced meal.
2. **Hydration:** ≥3 L water/day. 2 cups on waking; 1 cup before & after every meal (incl. snacks). Coffee ≤3/day, no sugar, water-based or ≤3% milk. Alcohol ≤1 glass (or ⅓ beer) once/week. Diet/zero drinks: allowed but discourage frequent use.
3. **Meal times:** keep to the listed windows. **Finish dinner by 20:00.**
4. **Avoid "red sticker" products** (very high sugar). Use "high saturated fat" / "high sodium" products only sparingly.
5. **Mindful eating:** sit down, no multitasking, eat from a plate not the package.
6. **Activity:** strength training is the base; add ≥1 session/week if possible; protect sleep and rest days.

## BREAKFAST — ארוחת בוקר
Recommended **07:00–08:00** (on a morning-training day: after the workout).

- **Option 1 (~25 g protein) [preferred]:** 2 hard-boiled eggs + ½ avocado + vegetables in 3 colors (cucumber / tomato / gamba pepper / radish / spinach) + 3 tbsp cottage cheese 5%.
- **Option 2 (~16 g protein):** 1 light pita + 2 slices yellow cheese 15% + vegetables (3 colors).
- **Option 3 (~10 g protein):** 1 cup goat yogurt + 1 tbsp sunflower seeds.
- ~~Smoked-salmon option~~ — **removed (fish).** Swap: 1 light pita + 1 hard-boiled egg + 1 slice yellow cheese 15% + 1 tbsp cream cheese 5% (~18 g protein).

## LUNCH — ארוחת צהריים
Recommended **12:00–13:30**.

- **Option 1 (~60 g protein) [preferred]:** 250–300 g oven-baked chicken breast + 4 tbsp brown rice + 1 cup steamed vegetables (broccoli / green beans / cauliflower / brussels sprouts) + 1 tbsp whole tahini thinned with water.
- **Option 2 (~60 g protein):** 250–300 g meatballs (grilled "baninja"/oven) + seasoned vegetable salad + 1 tsp olive oil + lemon juice.
- **Option 3 — vegetarian/vegan (~42 g protein):** 250–300 g stir-fried tofu + 1 tsp olive oil + 1 cup bean noodles + carrot + cucumber + 1 tbsp low-sodium soy sauce (no added sugar).
- ~~Baked-salmon option~~ — **removed (fish).** Swap: 250–300 g oven-baked turkey breast + 2 tbsp tomato-sauce pasta (no sugar) + 3 tbsp cooked peas (~60 g protein).

## INTERMEDIATE / SNACK — ארוחת ביניים
Once per day, at **10:00 or 17:00**.

- **Option 1 (~15 g protein) [preferred]:** plain yogurt ≤3% + 1 apple + 12 unroasted almonds.
- **Option 2 (~7 g protein):** cut vegetables (carrot / gamba / cucumber / celery) + 3 tbsp cottage cheese 5%.
- **Option 3 (~5 g protein):** 1 banana + 2 cubes 70%+ dark chocolate **or** 2 tsp sugar-free halva + 6 walnuts.
- **Option 4 (~9 g protein):** 2 whole-wheat crackers + 3 tsp whole tahini + 1 tsp honey.
- **Option 5 — training day (~23 g protein) [preferred on training days]:** 1 Pro yogurt 20 g protein **or** protein bar + 10 walnuts/natural almonds. (Alt: 1 Pro yogurt 20 g 1.5% + 20 walnuts/almonds.)

## DINNER — ארוחת ערב
Recommended **18:30–20:00** (on an evening-training day: after the workout, by 21:00).

- **Option 1 (~20 g protein) [preferred]:** 3 tbsp fine oats + ½ cup milk ≤3% + 1 tsp peanut butter.
- **Option 2 (~18 g protein):** 2 slices whole bread + shakshuka with 2 eggs + seasoned salad + 1 tsp olive oil + lemon juice.
- **Option 3 (~13 g protein):** 1 bowl vegetable soup with lentils.
- ~~Canned-tuna option~~ — **removed (fish).** Swap: 150 g grilled chicken/turkey strips + 1 tsp olive oil + vegetables in 3 colors (~30 g protein).

## NIGHT MEAL — ארוחת לילה
**Only on a training day, ~21:30.**

- **Option 1 (20 g protein):** Pro yogurt 20 g protein, no carb **or** half a protein bar.
- **Option 2 (12 g protein):** 2 hard-boiled eggs, whites only, no carb.
- **Option 3 (25 g protein):** protein shake on water, no fruit (alt: Pro yogurt 20 g 1.5% + 20 walnuts/almonds).

## WEEKENDS — סופי שבוע ("smart flexibility")
- **Friday:** meals are swapped — Friday dinner becomes the menu's "lunch"; **Friday evening is a free meal** (eat what you like, mindfully). Plan ahead: lighter lunch, no snacking, protein first then salads, take one portion not two/three, lots of water, less sweet drinks/alcohol. Finish relatively early, **by 21:00**.
- **Saturday (Shabbat):** a normal plan day like any other.

## HOLIDAYS — תוכנית חגים
1. Protein as the base of every meal; at holiday meals place protein first, then salads, carb last.
2. Don't arrive hungry — ~1 h before, eat a Pro yogurt or hard-boiled egg + 8–10 walnuts/almonds.
3. Recommended carbs: sweet potato, potato, brown rice, vegetable bakes.
4. Choose desserts/sweets wisely and plan them in advance.
5. Keep up water, training, and daily steps.
